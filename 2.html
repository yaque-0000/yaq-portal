Different Tiers of Imagery

First-hand: anything derived from the natural world, anything that is not man made

ex: tree, waterfall Second hand: something designed for functionality - not with the intention of it being art, emphasis is less on aesthetics, beauty, commentary, or an attempt to convey experience (not saying that functional items are not art) ex: fork, garbage can, cup

Third-hand: something that is designed with obvious consideration of aesthetics, visual purpose, implicit attempts at evoking emotion, e.g specific usage of colour, texture, imagery derived from first and secondhand images ex: advertisements, logos,
